Richest Vegetable in Beta-Carotene

Carrots contain more beta-carotene than almost any other vegetable. Your body converts it into Vitamin A — essential for vision, immunity and skin health.

02

93% of Your Daily Vitamin A

Just 100g of raw carrot provides 835 mcg RAE of Vitamin A — covering 93% of your entire daily requirement in a single serving.

03

Only 41 Calories per 100g

Despite being packed with nutrients, carrots are extremely low in calories. They are 88% water, making them one of the best foods for weight management.

04

Powerful Antioxidant Protection

Carrots contain polyacetylenes, lutein and zeaxanthin — antioxidants that protect your cells from damage, reduce inflammation and lower the risk of chronic disease.

05

Natural Blood Sugar Regulator

Despite their natural sweetness, carrots have a low glycemic index (GI of 16 raw). The fibre slows sugar absorption, making them safe and beneficial even for diabetics.

06

Boosts Absorption When Juiced

Juicing carrots breaks down the cell walls, making beta-carotene and other nutrients up to 6x more bioavailable — meaning your body absorbs far more nutrition from juice than raw.

Source: USDA National Nutrient Database & Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)

Health Benefits

Why Carrot is Nature's Superfood

Packed with vitamins, minerals and antioxidants — one glass of fresh carrot juice does wonders for your body

👁️

Improves Eyesight

Carrots are one of the best natural sources of beta-carotene and Vitamin A. These nutrients are essential for maintaining sharp, healthy vision, preventing night blindness, and reducing the risk of age-related eye diseases.

🛡️

Boosts Immunity

Rich in Vitamin C, Vitamin E and powerful antioxidants, carrots strengthen your immune system from the inside out. Regular intake helps your body fight infections, colds and seasonal illnesses naturally and effectively.

✨

Glows Your Skin

Beta-carotene in carrots converts to Vitamin A in the body, which actively repairs skin cells, reduces acne and blemishes, improves skin texture, and gives you a healthy natural glow from within — no filter needed!

❤️

Heart Health

Carrots are rich in potassium and antioxidants that help lower bad cholesterol (LDL), regulate blood pressure and reduce the risk of heart disease. A daily glass is one of the best natural gifts you can give your heart.

🧠

Brain Power

Lutein, zeaxanthin and beta-carotene in carrots protect brain cells from oxidative damage. Regular carrot juice consumption improves memory, sharpens focus and supports long-term cognitive health and mental clarity.

⚡

Natural Energy

Natural sugars, iron and B vitamins in fresh carrot juice give clean, sustained energy without any caffeine crash. Perfect as a morning booster, pre-workout drink or afternoon pick-me-up for lasting vitality all day.

🩸

Purifies Blood

Carrots act as a powerful natural blood purifier. The iron content improves haemoglobin levels, cleanses toxins from the blood and reduces fatigue — keeping your entire body functioning at its best every single day.

🦷

Strong Bones & Teeth

Calcium and Vitamin K found in carrots work together to strengthen your bones and teeth. Drinking carrot juice regularly helps prevent osteoporosis and keeps your skeletal system healthy, strong and resilient over time.

⚖️

Weight Management

Low in calories and high in fibre, carrots keep you feeling full for longer and support healthy digestion. A great natural addition to any weight loss or fitness routine — completely guilt-free and absolutely delicious.
